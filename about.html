About Us

Through collaboration and innovation, we open doors to international markets, uplifting SMEs and championing the aspirations of every individual.

RIFAH is a vibrant chamber of commerce in India, passionately dedicated to uplifting small and medium enterprises (SMEs), empowering minorities, socially oppressed classes, and championing women’s entrepreneurship. Founded in 2015 by a group of visionary entrepreneurs, RIFAH was born from a shared commitment to revamp the economic system. Our unique mission is to establish an interest-free financial landscape, fostering equitable growth across all communities and ensuring that capital is a catalyst for opportunity, not a tool of exploitation.

we are more than a chamber of commerce, we are a movement towards a future where every entrepreneur has the opportunity to succeed
